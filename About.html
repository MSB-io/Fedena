The Fedena Story

From a small idea to a global educational platform

Since
2009

Founded in 2009, Fedena began with a simple vision: to create an intuitive school management system that could work for educational institutions of all sizes. What started as a solution for a handful of local schools has evolved into a comprehensive platform used by over 40,000 institutions across 100+ countries.

Our team of educators, developers, and designers works tirelessly to ensure Fedena remains at the cutting edge of educational technology while maintaining its user-friendly approach that administrators, teachers, and students love.
