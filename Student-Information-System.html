What is the Student Information System (SIS)?

A comprehensive digital solution that automates data management and provides actionable insights for educational institutions worldwide.

Manually managing student data becomes a tiresome and vexing task for educators and administrators. With evolving technology in education, all operations can be easily streamlined, improving student productivity, efficiency & results.

With the Student Information System, institutes can manage an enormous amount of students' data in a single place with enhanced security in the most cost-effective way. Features such as admission processing, attendance tracking, performance analysis, parent communication, and much more can be seamlessly managed.

Our system not only reduces the workload of staff members but also ensures that institutes have complete control over their data, assisting faculties in monitoring student performance and making data-driven decisions to improve academic growth.

Key Features of Our Student Information System

Comprehensive tools designed to streamline operations and enhance the educational experience

Student Data Management

Manage all types of data, such as students' personal details, academic progress, documents, and attendance details. Admins can easily export the data anytime and make data-driven decisions.

Report Generation

Generate various comprehensive reports - attendance reports, fees pending reports, placement reports, examination result reports, and more. Customize and modify fields according to your institution's preferences.

Admission Management

Automate the entire admission process, reduce errors and paperwork. Efficiently manage student and parent details, customize admission forms, manage documents, and streamline the entire process.

Online Fees Payment

No more standing in long queues for fees submission. With Fedena, automate transactions, send instant alerts to parents/students on pending fees, add taxes and discounts according to your institution's requirements.

Transport Management

Real-time tracking of vehicle routing and stops with GPS integration. Manage and schedule transport fees collection. Also, ensure better safety and security for students during transit.

Attendance Management

Automate attendance tracking with biometric/RFID integration. Mark attendance subject-wise or day-wise, send instant alerts to parents, and keep them informed when their child misses classes.
