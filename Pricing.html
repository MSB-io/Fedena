Frequently Asked Questions

What is Fedena?

Fedena is an all-in-one school management system, which can be used to manage administrative and academic activities such as online admission management, fees, employees' payroll, examination, grade book, bulk data management, transportation, library, course and batches, student attendance, assignments, communication and much more. Find out more about Fedena.

How is Fedena different from other school management software?

Fedena stands out with its comprehensive feature set, unlimited user support, and flexible deployment options. It's designed for ease of use while offering deep customization to fit each institution's unique needs.

What kind of software programs do I require to run Fedena?

Fedena is a cloud-based solution, so you only need a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge) to access it. There's no need for additional software installation on your computers.

Can Fedena ERP software be customized for my Institute?

Yes, Fedena is highly customizable. We understand that each educational institution has unique requirements, and our platform can be tailored to match your specific workflows and processes.

Does Fedena provide data backup facility?

Yes, Fedena provides automated daily backups of all your data. These backups are encrypted and stored securely, ensuring your information remains protected and recoverable in case of any unforeseen events.
